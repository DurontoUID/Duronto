| Verified Purchase

This is not a slam against Wrangler (other than the fact that they are no longer made in the USA), I have worn them for years and have no argument with them. I chose my preferred size: 35x32, and Amazon suggested, based on customer reviews (or so they said) that I would be happier with 36x32. I am not happier with their suggestion. Taking into account that pre-washed jeans still shrink about 3 percent, I assumed that Amazon had data to back up their claim; it would appear not. My choice would have shrunk after wash to my perfect fit; these jeans are "falling off my butt" too large, working into the area of butt-crack territory even after a wash, and now its too late to return them. I have already washed them once and will now attempt to wash and dry them in the dryer - something I have never done, because I am not a savage. Bottom line: if you know your size do not be swayed by "suggestions". Go with what you know, and if you are in doubt, try them on before taking the tags off, and imagine a 3 percent shrinkage. You have worn jeans your entire life, you know how to judge size by now. If you don't, post a question, the Amazonian users are a knowledgeable group, they can help.
