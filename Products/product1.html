Product Description

The Laugh & Learn Smart Stages Piggy Bank introduces your baby to counting, colors, Spanish words and more through music, silly sounds and phrases. With 10 colorful coins to drop into the Piggy Bank’s back, and a door for put-and-take play, there’s so much for your little banker to explore. Put coins in, take them out, press the silly piggy’s snout – your baby will be giggling all the way to the bank! And with 2 Smart Stages levels of play, the learning fun grows along with your baby.
