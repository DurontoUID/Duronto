Emraan Sohail
| Verified Purchase

The watch is what I expected. All the features and faces are easy to navigate through and I like the app it syncs to on my phone. I have an android and the app works just fine. I have had a few glitches with the syncing to the app or where it has stopped counting steps in the middle of the day and I didn't notice it. Restarting the watch has fixed the sync issue or steps issue. I've had to do this twice in the 3 weeks I've had it. Otherwise, no complaints. The battery lasts a week which I was really impressed with. I wear it 24/7 (even in the shower) and only take it off to charge by my bedside while I'm sedentary. I like the BP, heartbeat, oxygen features and utilize the alarm to wake me up gently without waking my husband up. It even has a flashlight feature that I've found useless because it's not overly bright but bright enough to navigate a dark room or read a book at night with it. I got it to track my steps and see see approx. calories burned during walks/workouts so it's exceeded my needs and my expectations. The only reason I say it's not too smart is that it doesn't sync to my messages or social media, but I didn't want it to. I'm very satisfied with everything it can do and would buy again or recommend to any friend who wants these features and doesn't need to go above and beyond with a fitbit.
